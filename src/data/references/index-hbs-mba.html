The HBS MBA Experience

Our MBA program is designed to help students develop the skills and confidence they need to bring a fresh perspective to business.

The Case Method

Learn through the case method, which presents real-world business challenges and requires students to develop and defend solutions.

Learn more

Global Immersion

Gain international experience through global immersions, exchanges, and independent projects in locations around the world.

Learn more

Career Support

Access comprehensive career resources, including career coaching, industry treks, and networking opportunities with alumni.

Learn more

A Transformational Experience

The HBS MBA is more than a degree—it's a transformational experience that will shape your perspective and prepare you for leadership.

Develop Leadership Skills

Build confidence in your ability to lead through case discussions, team projects, and leadership development programs.

Expand Your Network

Join a global community of peers, faculty, and alumni who will support you throughout your career.

Gain Practical Experience

Apply classroom learning to real business challenges through field study projects and internships.

MBA Admissions

We seek students who demonstrate leadership potential, analytical aptitude, and a passion for making a difference in the world.

Application Deadlines

Round 1 September 5, 2024
Round 2 January 6, 2025
Round 3 April 8, 2025

Application Requirements

  • Completed online application
  • GMAT or GRE score
  • Academic transcripts
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Application essays
  • Interview (by invitation)
